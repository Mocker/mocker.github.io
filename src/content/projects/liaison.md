# Liaison

Liaison is a human-in-the-loop product discovery workspace. It separates confirmed facts from proposals, private engineering reasoning from published communication, and AI-generated drafts from human-approved decisions.

The current alpha demonstrates the full client-to-developer-to-client loop with deterministic browser state. Its production design uses Astro, React, TypeScript, Supabase, Cloudflare Workers, and provider-neutral AI adapters.
